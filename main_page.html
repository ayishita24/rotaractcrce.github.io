THEME OF THE YEAR

"Lucem" is a Latin word that carries the meaning of "light" in various contexts.

Light symbolises illumination, knowledge and understanding. Metaphorically, the term can be used to convey the idea of clarity, insight and enlightenment.

It's often employed to represent the dispelling of darkness or ignorance, highlighting the concept of gaining awareness and seeing things from a new perspective.

The term "Lucem" captures the powerful symbolism of light as a source of guidance and revelation.
